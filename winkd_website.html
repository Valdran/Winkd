About
Welcome to Winkd Messenger
Winkd brings back everything you loved about instant messaging — buddy lists, away messages, custom emoticons, and the legendary Winkd (Nudge) — rebuilt on modern infrastructure with end-to-end encryption. Free to download, no ads, no subscription, ever.
Version 1.0 is available for Windows Vista/7/10/11, Mac OS X, Linux, and Windows Mobile.
Key Features
💥 The Winkd (Nudge)
Shake a contact's window to get their attention. The most chaotic, beloved feature in messaging history — back where it belongs.
🔒 End-to-End Encrypted
All messages protected by Signal Protocol. Nobody reads your conversations — not even us.
🟢 Status & Away Messages
Online, Away, Busy, Invisible — plus a personal mood message your friends will actually read.
🎭 Buddy Icons & Display Names
Animated avatars, custom display names with colors and styles. Express yourself freely.
✨ Winks & Custom Emoticons
Full-screen animated Winks and custom emoticon packs. Create your own and share with friends.
🌐 Open Source & Federated
Run your own Winkd server. Full source code on GitHub. Your data, your rules.
